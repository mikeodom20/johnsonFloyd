J. Alex Johnson

Alex Johnson has been engaged in the practice of law in Baxley since 1978. Prior to establishing his practice here, he was associated with Calhoun First National Bank and a law firm in Calhoun, Georgia. Johnson's practice has ranged from a sole practitioner to a five member firm with offices in Baxley and neighboring Hazlehurst.

Johnson received a BBA in Finance from the University of Georgia College of Business Administration. He is also a graduate of the University's School of Law, where he was a member of the Georgia Law Review. Johnson is admitted to practice in all Georgia state and federal courts.

His areas of practice are business, real estate, banking, corporate, probate, creditor bankruptcy, and municipal law. He has served as City Attorney and County Development Authority Attorney for some twenty-five years. Johnson was elected as a director of the former Baxley State Bank in 1982 and continued to serve as an advisory director after its acquisition by Trust Company Bank. He was also a member of the board of directors of SunTrust Bank of Southeast Georgia (Brunswick, Waycross, Jesup, Baxley) for eighteen years; and is now a director of the Community Bank of Georgia.

Johnson was elected three times as Solicitor of the State Court of Appling County, serving from 1983 to 1993, and has served as Judge of the Juvenile Court of Appling County since 1993. He is a former vice president of the Georgia Trial Lawyers Association; and Bar activities include former service on the Disciplinary Panel and currently on the Board of Governors, serving on various committees. He has also held numerous church, civic and community leadership positions.
He is married with two adult sons and one daughter-in-law.
